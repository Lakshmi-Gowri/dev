About Me

Hi! I'm Lakshmi Gowri, a passionate Software Engineer & Machine Learning Enthusiast in building scalable applications. My journey began in full-stack development, where I honed my skills in React.js, Node.js, and Python, but my curiosity for AI and Machine Learning led me to specialize in deep learning, recommendation systems, and predictive analytics. Currently pursuing an M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning from BITS Pilani, I thrive on solving complex problems with data-driven solutions. Beyond coding, I actively participate in HackerEarth ML competitions, contribute to open-source AI projects, and stay updated on emerging trends in Deep Learning & AI Ethics. I'm always open to collaboration, whether it's in machine learning research, AI product development, or software engineering.
